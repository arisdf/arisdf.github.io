How to drive a nail into a steel plate?

> Back: Mars Task

To drive nails into steel plates, professional tools and methods are usually required. Due to the high hardness of steel plates, ordinary hammers and nails are difficult to complete. It is recommended to use the following steps:

Pre-drilling with an electric drill:

First, use a drill bit suitable for the diameter of the nail to drill holes in the steel plate, which can reduce subsequent resistance.

Select special steel nails:

such as self-tapping screws or cement steel nails, whose hardness should be higher than that of steel plate material.

Impact tool fixation:

Apply instantaneous high pressure using a nail gun or an electric nail gun, or achieve it by hammering expansion bolts.

Pay attention to wearing goggles and make sure there is a support on the back of the steel plate to prevent deformation.
