What exactly is soul?

> Back: Think Soul

The soul is a complex concept that spans philosophy, religion and science. In Plato's theory, the soul is the eternal carrier of ideas. Buddhism holds that it is the continuous flow of "consciousness". Modern neuroscience, on the other hand, tends to explain related phenomena through consciousness and cognitive functions. Perhaps it can be understood in this way: The soul is a poetic inquiry into the essence of human existence, encompassing both biological neural activities and symbolic meanings bestowed by culture.
